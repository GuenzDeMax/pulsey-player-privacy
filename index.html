Permissions Breakdown

Pulsey Player requires the following permissions to function properly:

Required Permissions

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Allows Pulsey Player to access and play your local music files stored on your device
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Used for caching playback data and saving app preferences locally
  • INTERNET: Required to load and display advertisements from Google AdMob
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Enables continuous music playback even when the app is minimized or screen is off
What We DON'T Request:
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Precise location tracking
  • SMS or phone permissions

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to safeguard the data handled by our app and its third-party integrations. This includes:

  • Encrypted data transmission when communicating with ad services
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Compliance with Google Play security requirements
Please Note:

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Pulsey Player is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided us with personal information should contact us immediately.
