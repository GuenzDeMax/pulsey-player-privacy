Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 10, 2025

Last Updated: January 10, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Pulsey Player. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our offline music player application handles your data.

Key Point: Pulsey Player is a completely offline music player. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

Information We Access

To provide you with music playback functionality, Pulsey Player requires access to:

1. Storage Permission

  • Purpose: To scan and access audio files stored on your device
  • What we access: Audio files (MP3, M4A, FLAC, WAV, OGG formats) on your device storage
  • What we do: Read metadata (song title, artist, album, artwork) from audio files
  • Data handling: All data stays on your device

2. Audio/Media Files

  • Song titles, artist names, album names
  • Album artwork embedded in audio files
  • Audio file locations and durations
  • File modification dates

How We Use Information

All information accessed by Pulsey Player is used solely for:

  • Displaying your music library
  • Playing audio files
  • Organizing songs by artist, album, and folder
  • Caching library data locally for faster app loading
  • Creating and managing playlists

Data Storage

100% Local Storage:

  • All app data is stored only on your device
  • Music metadata cache is stored using Android SharedPreferences
  • Playlists and app settings are stored locally
  • No data is transmitted to any external servers
  • No cloud synchronization or backup to external services

Data Sharing

We do not share any data. Period.

Pulsey Player does not:

  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Share your music library information with third parties
  • Sell or rent your information
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Display advertisements
  • Require user accounts or login

Third-Party Services

Pulsey Player does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app is completely self-contained and offline.

Children's Privacy

Pulsey Player does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any users. As the app does not collect data at all, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Security

Since all data remains on your device:

  • Your music library data is as secure as your device
  • We recommend using device lock screens and encryption
  • App data can be cleared through Android settings if needed

Permissions Breakdown

Required Permissions:

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To read audio files from your device
  • MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 11+): To access audio files across all storage locations

We DO NOT request:

  • Internet permission
  • Location access
  • Camera access
  • Contacts access
  • Call logs or phone state

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the app (removes all cached data)
  • Using "Clear Data" in Android app settings
  • Using the "Force Rescan" feature within the app to rebuild the cache

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or transmit your data, you retain complete control over your information:

  • All data stays on your device
  • You can revoke storage permissions at any time
  • You can delete the app and all its data anytime

Summary

In Simple Terms:

Pulsey Player is a completely offline music player. We only access your music files to play them. Everything stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. Ever.
